About Me

As an aspiring data analyst,I am driven by a passion for harnessing the power of data to drive positive change. My journey began in 2021 when I had the opportunity to collect and report findings on enviornmental data for a construction project. This experience led me to embark on a path of self-learning in the world of data analytics. Eager to expand my skills, I enrolled in an online professional certificate course , which equipped me with a solid foundation in data analysis, including the art of asking insightful questions and effectively conveying findings through impactful visualizations.

Continuing my professional growth, I am currently immersed in self-learning endeavors, focusing on mastering languages such as Python to enhance my data manipulation and analysis capabilities.Through personal projects and ongoing learning, I am dedicated to further developing my abilities as a data analyst and breaking into the industry. With a strong commitment to leveraging data-driven insights, I am excited to contribute my skills and make a meaningful impact in the field of data analysis.
